🐾 PawPal Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

Quick Summary

What we collect: Pet info, health records, photos you provide

Where it's stored: On your device only (SwiftData)

Do we share it: No, we don't share your data

How to delete it: Uninstall the app

Introduction

Welcome to PawPal. We are committed to protecting your privacy and the information you provide while using our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Pet Information: Names, species, breed, weight, photos
  • Health Records: Vet records, vaccinations, medications, allergies
  • Contact Information: Owner name, phone, email, emergency contacts
  • Veterinarian Information: Vet clinic details

Data Storage

Important: All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData. We do NOT store your information on our servers.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Emergency QR Codes

Note: The QR code feature lets you share your pet's info with others. Only include information you're comfortable sharing publicly. The QR code can be scanned by anyone who has access to it.

Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to delete all data
  • Control: You control what info is in QR codes

Medical Disclaimer

PawPal is not a substitute for professional veterinary care. The information provided is for informational purposes only. Always consult a licensed veterinarian for medical concerns about your pet.

Children's Privacy

PawPal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
