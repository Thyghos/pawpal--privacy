Privacy Policy for Petpal

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Introduction

Petpal (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information when you use the Petpal mobile app.

What information the app may use

  • Pet information — name, species, breed, weight, date of birth, photos
  • Health & care — reminders, notes, emergency profile details you enter
  • Location — only when you use features that find nearby places (e.g. pet-friendly locations). Used on your device for that session as allowed by your OS.
  • Photos — when you choose a profile photo from your library or camera

How your data is stored

Most information you enter in Petpal is stored locally on your device using Apple’s on-device storage (SwiftData). We do not operate our own servers to collect or store your pet profiles, health notes, or emergency data.

Device backups (e.g. iCloud or computer backup) are managed by Apple; see Apple’s Privacy Policy for how backups work.

AI features

If you use any AI-assisted features, your questions or prompts may be sent to a third-party AI provider to generate responses. Do not include sensitive personal or medical data you would not want processed by such a service. AI output is informational only and is not a substitute for a veterinarian.

Third-party services

The app may use services such as mapping or place search when you use location-related features. Those providers process requests according to their own privacy policies.

Children

Petpal is not directed at children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, contact us and we will help you remove it from your device.

Your choices

  • You can delete app data by deleting the app or clearing data within the app where available.
  • You can deny photo, camera, or location access in your device settings; some features may not work without them.

Medical disclaimer

Petpal is not medical or veterinary advice. Always consult a licensed veterinarian for health decisions about your pet.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do.
