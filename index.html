Quick Summary

What we collect: Pet info, health records, photos you provide

Where it's stored: On your device only (SwiftData)

Do we share it: No, we don't share your data

How to delete it: Uninstall the app

Introduction

Welcome to PetPal ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and the information you provide while using our mobile application.

This Privacy Policy explains what information PetPal collects, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use PetPal, you may provide the following information:

  • Pet Information: Pet names, species, breed, weight, date of birth, and photos
  • Health Records: Veterinary records, vaccination information, medical conditions, medications, and allergies
  • Contact Information: Owner name, phone number, email address, and emergency contacts
  • Veterinarian Information: Vet clinic names, addresses, and phone numbers
  • Care Instructions: Feeding schedules, special needs, and care notes

Automatically Collected Information

PetPal may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Usage Data: Features used, crashes, and performance metrics (if analytics are enabled)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Provide App Functionality: Store and organize your pet's health records
  • Enable Features: Create Emergency QR codes, set reminders, track appointments
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Customer Support: Respond to your questions and support requests

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Important: All your personal data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not store your personal information on our servers.

Your data includes:

  • Pet profiles and photos
  • Health records and documents
  • Reminders and appointments
  • Travel plans and checklists

Data Backup

Your data may be backed up through:

  • iCloud: If you have iCloud backup enabled on your device
  • iTunes/Finder: If you perform device backups on your computer

These backups are managed by Apple, not by PetPal. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how Apple handles your data.

Security Measures

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Local encryption through iOS security features
  • No transmission of personal data to external servers
  • Secure storage using Apple's SwiftData framework

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We Do NOT Share Your Data

PetPal does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Third-Party Services

PetPal uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple Services: For device functionality, push notifications, and iCloud sync

If you choose to use these features, Apple's privacy policy applies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Delete Your Data: You can delete individual records or all data by uninstalling the app
  • Control Sharing: You decide what information to include in Emergency QR codes

How to Delete Your Data

To delete all your data:

  1. Uninstall the PetPal app from your device
  2. If you have iCloud backup enabled, you may need to delete the backup separately

Children's Privacy

PetPal is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Emergency QR Code Feature

Important: The Emergency QR Code feature allows you to create a QR code containing your pet's medical information that can be scanned by others.

Only include information you are comfortable sharing publicly. The QR code can be scanned by anyone who has access to it.

Information that may be shared via QR code:

  • Pet name and species
  • Medical conditions and allergies
  • Emergency contact information
  • Veterinarian information
  • Medications

You control what information is included in the QR code.

AI-Powered Features (Vet AI)

If you use the Vet AI feature:

  • Conversations may be processed by AI service providers
  • We do not store conversation history
  • The AI provides general information, not professional veterinary advice
  • Always consult a licensed veterinarian for medical concerns

Medical Disclaimer

PetPal is not a substitute for professional veterinary care.

The information provided by PetPal, including any AI-powered features, is for informational purposes only and should not be considered medical advice.

Always consult a licensed veterinarian for medical concerns about your pet.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as you have the app installed on your device. When you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is deleted (unless backed up to iCloud/iTunes).

International Users

PetPal is designed for use worldwide. Your data is stored locally on your device, regardless of your location.

If your data is backed up to iCloud, it may be stored in Apple's data centers in various countries. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (We do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectify inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure (right to be forgotten)
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
